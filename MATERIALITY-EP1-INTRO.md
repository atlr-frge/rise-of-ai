## Production notes for this episode

- **Shoot Michael once:** cold open + sign-off are the same setup, same outfit, same light.
  Two takes, ~30 seconds of usable footage total. That's the entire human shoot.
- **Source documents must be real and current** (FERC queue, an analyst note, a 10-K power
  line). Citation-disciplined per the deck — every on-screen number gets its mono tag.
- **One Circle, one Strike minimum** — this episode has two Strikes (benchmark, model leaderboard)
  and two Circles (the wait time, the verdict). Don't add more; one judgment per frame.
- **B-roll sourcing:** substations / transmission / cooling plants, all graded to the single
  house LUT, dirtied with grain. Reuse this plate library across the series.
- **Compliance:** the "noise/material" verdict is editorial opinion on what's *mispriced*,
  not investment advice — keep the standard disclaimer in the caption/end card, Canaccord-cleared.
