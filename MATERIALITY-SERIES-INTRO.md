# Materiality — Series Trailer (the foundation piece)

*The piece that lays the foundation and sets up all five episodes.*
*Knock-off of the @emilyissocial countdown format: "There are 5 [things] — the last one changes everything."*
*Format: Hybrid · 9:16 vertical · target 45–60s · built on [MATERIALITY-PRODUCTION-SYSTEM.md](MATERIALITY-PRODUCTION-SYSTEM.md).*

> **Why this format:** Emily's verified hook on that clip is *"There are 5 stages to AI — the
> last one changes everything."* That's a countdown + curiosity-gap structure, and it maps
> onto Materiality perfectly because the series already **is** five forces. We steal the
> structure and the kinetic energy; we reframe the payoff so the "thing that changes
> everything" is **the act of judging what's material** — which is the entire thesis.
